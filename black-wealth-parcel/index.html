Community Update

Bringing the Invest in My Education (ME)SM initiative to life

Announced in January 2023, Invest in My Education (ME)SM is a $250 million social impact initiative designed to help Black, Latino, and other historically underserved students pay for college, improve economic mobility, and build generational wealth. The program combines scholarships with ongoing assistance, mentorship, and pathways to post-graduation employment. If you would like to receive more information about the program or want to be notified when the next round of applications opens, contact us
